Whole Person Healing Center is a collaborative wellness space for body, mind, and spirit - supporting people across all stages of life.

Located in Myrtle Beach next to Myrtle Beach Mall, Whole Person Healing Center offers a trusted space where clients can access personalized integrative care under one roof, and private office rentals for licensed and certified health professionals. We believe healing happens best in connection, and that everyone deserves to feel at home on their path to wellness.

Why Choose Whole Person Healing Center?

Whole Person Healing Center is dedicated to providing an integrative approach to healthcare, offering a wide range of holistic services and experienced practitioners. We focus on treating the mind, body, and spirit to promote overall well-being.
