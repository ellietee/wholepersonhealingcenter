Healing for the Whole Person

At Whole Person Healing Center, we believe true healing addresses body, mind, heart, and spirit. Our integrated team of practitioners offers evidence-based and time-honored modalities to support your journey toward wellness, vitality, and wholeness.

✨ Energy Healing
▼

Energy healing works with the body's subtle energy systems (chakras, meridians, and auric field) to remove blockages, restore balance, and activate your innate healing capacity. Through intuitive guidance and hands-on or distance techniques, practitioners help you return to a state of wholeness and vitality. Offered by: Dineen Carta, Zahra Ghazi

🔊 Sound & Vibrational Therapy
▼

Sound therapy uses the healing power of frequency, vibration, and resonance to release tension, calm the nervous system, and promote deep healing. Through Tibetan bowls, tuning forks, chimes, voice, and PEMF devices, sound reaches the deepest layers of body and consciousness. Offered by: Dineen Carta, Zahra Ghazi, Deb Smith, Mindi Stronzik

💆 Therapeutic Massage & Bodywork
▼

From clinical massage and trigger point therapy to integrative bodywork, our massage practitioners use skilled touch to release tension, improve circulation, reduce pain, and restore ease to the body. Many sessions incorporate modalities like lymphatic drainage, acupressure, and PEMF technology for enhanced healing. Offered by: Mindi Stronzik, Deb Smith

🧘 Movement & Somatic Practices
▼

Gentle, conscious movement practices like the Alexander Technique and yoga help you reconnect with your body's natural poise, balance, and coordination. These practices build awareness, release held tension, and restore the body's innate wisdom—supporting both physical healing and emotional well-being. Offered by: Lenita C. Silveira, Zahra Ghazi

🧠 Meditation & Mindfulness
▼

Guided meditation and mindfulness practices calm the mind, reduce stress and anxiety, and deepen your connection to inner peace and clarity. Through breath work, guided imagery, and present-moment awareness, these practices support emotional resilience and spiritual awakening. Offered by: Zahra Ghazi, Dr. Elle Todd

💫 Reiki Energy Healing
▼

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice that supports the body's natural healing responses. Through hands-on or distance techniques, Reiki practitioners channel healing energy to release blockages, reduce stress, and promote balance across physical, emotional, and spiritual dimensions. Offered by: Meheru McPherson, Dr. Elle Todd

💚 Life Coaching & Transformational Guidance
▼

Life coaching and transformational coaching support you through life changes, challenges, and transitions. Coaches provide guidance, accountability, and practical tools to help you clarify your path, overcome obstacles, and walk forward with confidence and grace. Offered by: Dineen Carta

🏥 Physical Therapy & Wellness Coaching
▼

Our licensed Doctor of Physical Therapy offers integrative care combining clinical expertise with holistic approaches. Services include injury recovery, chronic pain management, movement education, personal training, and comprehensive wellness coaching to help you rebuild strength, resilience, and vitality. Offered by: Dr. Elle Todd

🦴 Chiropractic & Structural Alignment
▼

Advanced chiropractic care combining structural alignment with energetic and neurological approaches. Through Network Spinal Analysis and Sacro Occipital Technique, practitioners help restore proper alignment, improve nervous system function, and support your body's innate healing capacity. Offered by: Dr. Jim Oppenheim

🔮 Holistic Mental Health & Psychotherapy
▼

Integrative mental health services that merge clinical psychology with holistic modalities like Bio-Intrinsic Transformational Therapy™, Past Life Regression, and energy medicine. Comprehensive support for emotional healing, trauma recovery, substance abuse, and spiritual growth. Offered by: Dr. Robin Reeves-Oppenheim

🏡 Feng Shui & Environmental Healing
▼

Feng Shui consultation and environmental design create spaces that support healing, balance, and well-being. By harmonizing energy flow and intentional placement, your home becomes a sanctuary that supports your wellness journey and nurtures your whole-person health. Offered by: Meheru McPherson

🌟 Depth Psychology & Spiritual Direction
▼

Explore the depths of consciousness, soul wisdom, and spiritual awakening through Jungian meditation, depth psychology, and spiritual direction. These practices guide inner transformation, self-discovery, and reconnection with your authentic purpose and wholeness. Offered by: Zahra Ghazi
