The Heart Behind WPHC

With over four decades of experience in integrative mind-body-spirit approaches to physical, emotional, psychological, and spiritual well-being, Dr. Robin and Dr. Jim founded Whole Person Healing Center to be a place of connection, collaboration, and care. Their vision continues to shape WPHC as a supportive home for clients and practitioners alike on the path to healing.

Dr. Robin Reeves-Oppenheim

Dr. Robin Reeves-Oppenheim, ThD, LICSW-CP, LCSW, DCSW, BC-TMH, CHT, is a distinguished expert and author with over 42 years of clinical experience in mental health and substance abuse. As the creator of Bio-Intrinsic Transformational Therapy™, she merges Eastern and Western mysticism, Psychology, Energy Medicine, Quantum Physics, Neurotheology, and Biofield Therapies to foster holistic healing. She is the co-owner of Whole Person Healing Center and the President and CEO of Out-of-the-Box Solutions, Inc. in Georgia. Dr. Reeves-Oppenheim has served on numerous professional boards, trained with Dr. Brian Weiss in Past Life Regression Therapy, and presented internationally on spirituality and wellness. She is on the Advisory Board of the National Organization for Medical Intuition and a faculty member of HOLOS University. Dedicated to transforming lives, she integrates innovative therapeutic models to empower individuals on their path to healing and self-discovery.

Dr. Jim Oppenheim

Dr. Jim Oppenheim, DC, MPH, is a seasoned Chiropractor, educator, and innovator with 45 years of experience. With advanced training in Network Spinal Analysis and Sacro Occipital Technique, Dr. Oppenheim integrates structural, energetic, and neurological approaches to support deep healing and alignment. He is the co-owner of Whole Person Healing Center and a respected member of both the International Craniopathic Society and the Sacro Occipital Research Society International. Dr. Oppenheim holds a Doctor of Chiropractic from Life University and a Master of Public Health in Health Administration from the University of South Carolina. Based on over four decades of experience with cutting-edge, evidence-based chiropractic care, he brings a heart-centered approach that has empowered thousands of clients and students to reconnect with their innate healing capacity. His mission is to serve the community with compassion, excellence, and a spirit of unwavering commitment to growth.
